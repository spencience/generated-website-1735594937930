Behind the World's Biggest Burger

The Making of a Legend

Our record-breaking burger wasn't just a feat of cooking - it was a masterpiece of engineering and culinary expertise. Created by our team of 35 expert chefs, the burger required specialized equipment including a custom-built grill the size of a swimming pool.

Key Achievements

  • Guinness World Record holder since 2023
  • Featured on international food networks
  • Served over 10,000 people at a charity event
  • Zero food waste - all leftovers donated

Ingredients Breakdown

  • 🧀 300 lbs melted cheese
  • 🥬 200 lbs fresh lettuce
  • 🍅 150 lbs ripe tomatoes
  • 🧅 100 lbs onions
  • 🥖 Custom-baked 500 lb bun
