Our Journey to Breaking Records

2019: The Dream Begins

What started as a crazy idea in our founder's kitchen turned into a mission to create something extraordinary. Chef Mike "Big Patty" Patterson gathered a team of passionate burger enthusiasts.

2020: The First Attempt

Our first attempt reached 1,200 pounds - impressive but not enough. We learned valuable lessons about structural integrity and heat distribution.

2021: Engineering Innovation

We partnered with food scientists and engineers to design custom cooking equipment and develop our signature support structure.

2023: World Record Success

Finally, success! Our 2,014-pound burger broke the world record, requiring a custom-built grill and 35 skilled chefs working in perfect coordination.
