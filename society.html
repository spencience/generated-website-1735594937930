Social Structure

The Culture represents the pinnacle of post-scarcity anarchist society, where artificial intelligences known as Minds handle most administrative and productive tasks. Citizens enjoy complete freedom of self-determination, with no formal hierarchy or economic constraints.

Daily Life

Culture citizens typically live lives of enlightened hedonism, pursuing personal interests, creative endeavors, and social relationships. Body modification, virtual reality, and space travel are common recreational activities.

Governance & Decision Making

While the Culture has no formal government, decisions affecting large groups are made through a combination of AI oversight and direct democratic consensus. Special Circumstances serves as an informal diplomatic and interventionist arm.

Ethics & Values

The Culture's ethical framework emphasizes individual freedom, rational hedonism, and responsible intervention in less developed civilizations. The society maintains a complex relationship with its AI partners and ongoing debates about interventionism.
