Culture Technology Overview

Gridfire

Weaponized hyperspace technology capable of destroying entire solar systems. Primarily deployed by Culture warships.

Effectors

Multipurpose force field manipulators used for everything from manufacturing to warfare.

Neural Laces

Brain-computer interfaces that allow direct neural connections and mind state backups.

Displacement Engines

FTL propulsion systems utilizing hyperspace fields for near-instantaneous travel.

Force Fields

Energy field technology used for everything from personal protection to habitat construction.

Ship Minds

Hyperintelligent AIs that control and maintain Culture ships and habitats.

Technology Classification

The Culture classifies technology on a logarithmic scale from Tech Level 1 to 8, with most Culture technology operating at Level 8. Access detailed analysis of specific technologies via the links above.
